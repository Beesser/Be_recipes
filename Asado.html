Description

For a good asado, we need three main things: GOOD meat and ENOUGH woodfire to prepare it and the most important, NICE PPL!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Wood
  • Matambre
  • Vacio
  • Costilla
  1. Grab some old newspaper and small stickts to make enough flame to turn goodfire
  2. Once, the fire is good, grab a good knife, chopboard, salt, pepper and the meat
  3. Now is time to take small chank of fat that won't be necessary and salt pepper
  4. If we have achuras, this is the time for place them in a bowl with lemon juice
  5. Place the grill on the fire, wait 1,2 minutes and clean it with newspaper
  6. Grab a good spoon of charcol and place it all along the same side of the grill and place it on top
  7. Now carefully place the meat on the grill paying special attention on the fire, the tight parts will require low fire and stay on the grill longer than others cuts
